* Keyboard arrow keys:
        Left/Right -- Move camera Clockwise/Anticlockwise
                      around Tux.
        Up/Down    -- Move camera closer/further from 
        
        '['/']'    -- Move camera Clockwise/Anticlockwise
                      around Tux.
        
* Keyboard Ins/Del cluster:
        PgUp/PgDown-- Move camera higher/lower relative to
                      Tux.
        Home       -- Reset camera to defaults.
        End        -- Toggle camera between following behind
                      Tux or moving parallel to him. This one 
                      is very recommended in hard levels.
        Insert     -- Toggle wide field of view/narrow field
                      of view.
        Delete     -- Quickly move all the way in close or
                      further away.

        ';'        -- Move camera by WASD or mouse motion.
         '         -- Rotate camera by WASD or mouse motion.

* Main keyboard:
        WASD     -- self-explanatory
        SPACE    -- Toggle Menu - also gets rid of on-screen messages.
        'r'      -- Roll.
        'h'      -- Jump, or Swim.
        'j'      -- Dive, or Throw.
        'p'      -- Toggle Pause mode.
        'z'      -- Fast-Forward. Just like in emulators.
        ESC      -- Exit game.
        Ctrl+C   -- Exit game.
        F1       -- Enable Help screen
        F2       -- Enable About screen
        notice something missing? there is a... whole word...

      CHEAT MODE CONTROLS:

        'x'      -- Exit cheat mode
        'k'      -- This - ultimate mode - What they call "Kludge mode" - Toggle "move Tux using number keys" sub-mode.
                      1/2     -- Move in X
                      ./ENTER -- Move in Y
                      -/+     -- Move in Z
                      4/6     -- Change heading
                      7/9     -- Change roll
                      8/2     -- Change pitch
                       5      -- Stop rotation
                       0      -- Stop all motion
        '('      -- Toggle debug "console", do not press, or crash imminent.
        ')'      -- Display Tux's coordinates on console. (it wont show unless the very unstable '(' keyboard button has been pressed)
        '_'      -- Skip to next level.
        '='      -- Go back one level.
        '*'      -- Toggle texture on and off.
        '&'      -- Toggle wireframe on and off.
        '^'      -- Stop enemy critters and other animations from moving.
        '%'      -- Recharge lives, herring, air, health, snowballs,etc. (or some people call it beginner pack)
